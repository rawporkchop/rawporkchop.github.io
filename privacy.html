Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

AVR Link does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app talks only to your own AV receiver on your local network, and to your own paired devices — nothing goes further than that.

What we collect

Nothing. AVR Link has no server, no account system, and no analytics. We — the developer — never receive any information about you, your receiver, or how you use the app.

What the app connects to

AVR Link's whole purpose is talking to hardware you own, over a network you control. Specifically:

  • Your Denon/Marantz receiver — the macOS, iOS, and watchOS apps discover and control your receiver directly over your local network (via Bonjour discovery and a local telnet connection to the receiver). This traffic stays on your LAN and never reaches us.
  • Your other Apple devices — the Apple Watch app relays commands through your paired iPhone using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework, and the iPhone, its widgets, and Live Activities share status through a private App Group container. Both are local, on-device (or on-network) mechanisms provided by iOS/watchOS — no data passes through any server we operate.

Local storage

The app caches certain data — such as known receivers, per-zone volume limits, and display preferences — directly on your device(s). This data:

  • Never leaves your devices or your local network
  • Is not visible to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared from the app's Settings, or removed entirely by deleting the app

Third parties

AVR Link does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking software of any kind. No third-party SDKs collect data on our behalf. The only "third parties" involved are your own receiver and your own Apple devices, as described above.

Children's privacy

Because AVR Link collects no data from anyone, it does not collect data from children either. The app is safe for use by any age group with respect to privacy.

Changes to this policy

If our data practices ever change, this page will be updated, and the "last updated" date above will reflect the change. We encourage you to check back if you have concerns.

Contact

Questions about this policy or how the app works? Reach out at contact@avrlink.app or open an issue on GitHub.
